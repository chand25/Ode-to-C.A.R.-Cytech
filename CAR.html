Cyborgs. Androids. Robots.

Types of Simicrants

Cyborgs are made of both organic and biomechatronic body parts primarily human augmentation and enhancement.

Androids are humanoid robots or synthetic organisms designed to look and act more like a human. They are equipped with artificial intelligence software (A.I.) technology.

Robots are made of mechanical parts and the designs are based on their intended function. They do not necessarily have to be in the form of a human, whereas an android is always in the form of a human.
